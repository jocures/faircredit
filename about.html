Our Principles

We operate on three core principles--compassion, innovation, and transparency. FariCredit’s goal is to help people. We are working to create innovative platforms for people to reduce their debt and rebuild their credit. We will be offering a variety of online platforms to seamlessly reduce a person’s debt and rebuild their credit. We will offer financial education and be able to have the opportunity to be able to provide capital to our customers.

Our Philosophy

FairCredit is developing a debt reduction system to help people with delinquent debt repair their credit. This is accomplished by forgiving up to 80% of your debt and then utilizing a recurrent payment model to pay whatever remains. We want to work with you to set up a payment system that best suits your needs. This repayment method will give you the opportunity to rebuild your credit. This leads us to our ultimate goal of providing you with capital. When your credit is back in good standing, you will have the opportunity to get back into the economy.
